<h1 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Stonevaults: Dungeons &amp; Towers</strong></h1>
<p>Venture into a world filled with mysterious towers, hidden igloos, and dangerous dungeons waiting to be explored.</p>
<p>Each structure contains unique rooms, loot, puzzles, and challenges. Fight your way through relentless waves of monsters, uncover hidden treasures, and survive encounters around every corner. Whether you're climbing towering fortresses, descending into forgotten underground complexes, or exploring frozen ruins, every expedition offers new rewards and dangers.</p>
<hr>
<p>Dwelve into towers, dungeons, and igloos. Fight your way through, break all puzzles with a pickaxe, retrieve rewards, and hide in corners as monsters attack you non-stop.</p>
<hr>
<h1 style="text-align: center;"><strong>Features</strong></h1>
<ul>
<li>&nbsp;Procedurally generated Mage Towers</li>
<li>&nbsp;Frozen Igloos packed with secrets</li>
<li>&nbsp;Dangerous Dungeons filled with enemies</li>
<li>&nbsp;Pillager strongholds and challenges</li>
<li>&nbsp;Valuable loot and hidden rewards</li>
<li>&nbsp;Puzzle rooms and exploration challenges</li>
<li>&nbsp;Intense combat encounters</li>
</ul>
<p>Can you conquer every structure, claim its treasures, and survive the endless monsters lurking within?</p>
<p>Explore. Fight. Loot. Survive.</p>
